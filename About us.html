Our Background and History

HueCafé Bar was founded in 1999 by Christian Hubert "Hue" Decasa, a passionate coffee enthusiast with a vision to create a truly unique café experience in Bacoor, Cavite. Hue's love for coffee and dedication to providing exceptional quality led him on a journey to source and serve only the finest imported beans from around the world. Driven by a desire to share his passion for coffee, Hue embarked on a mission to create a space where people could gather, relax, and indulge in the art of coffee appreciation. With meticulous attention to detail, he carefully designed the café bar to reflect his vision, combining elements of comfort, style, and a welcoming ambiance. As the word spread about HueCafé Bar's dedication to serving outstanding coffee, the establishment quickly gained popularity among locals and visitors alike. The café bar became a hub for coffee enthusiasts, offering a haven where they could savor the rich aromas and flavors of meticulously brewed beverages. Hue's commitment to excellence extended beyond coffee alone. Recognizing the importance of creating a holistic experience, he expanded the menu to include a diverse range of snacks and delectable treats that perfectly complemented the coffee offerings. Each item on the menu was carefully curated and crafted to ensure that every guest's palate was satisfied.

Over the years, HueCafé Bar has grown from a local favorite to a beloved establishment known for its exceptional quality and warm hospitality. Despite its expansion and success, the café bar has remained true to its roots, with Hue's guiding philosophy at the core of every aspect of the business. Our expansion to multiple branches in Cavite, Manila, Alabang, and Tagaytay is a testament to the growing popularity and success of our café.

We strive to continuously innovate and introduce new flavors and brewing techniques to satisfy the evolving tastes of our customers. At HueCafé Bar, we believe that great coffee is not just a beverage but an experience to be savored and shared.
